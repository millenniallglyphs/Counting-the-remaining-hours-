We have

years days hours minutes seconds

to adapt to catostrophic climate change.

In that time you'll

grow inches of hair

eat meals

and take steps.

Though the time is quickly rolling, We can develop strategies of survival, mindful of our needs and the needs of our non-human companions. There is only a fraction of a generation remaining. We cannot wait. If there is no one to follow, we must learn to be our own ancestors.
